I designed an Offgrid Hybrid Solar-Wind System for a village without Electricity. My career is dedicated to achieving stable, reliable, and eco-friendly power for areas in Ghana, combining quantitative and field skills to answer power-related questions immediately relevant to a better power network.

I am currently a contractor at the Electricity Company of Ghana, where I work with the Research & Development Division to solve Engineering problems. I completed my Bachelor's Degree with KNUST in Ghana, Kumasi.
